How to Unlock Your Device Remotely

To receive credit for the device you traded-in, it is important to follow the steps below within XX days of receiving your first text message. Because you no longer have the device, you will need to remotely erase it and then remove it from iCloud. Follow the steps below to get started.

Steps for accessing from an Apple mobile device

Accessing from your desktop or non-Apple device?

Step One

Select “Open Find My iPhone”

on https://www.icloud.com/#find

You may be prompted to login with your Apple ID and password.

Step Two

Select the device that you traded in and Tap Actions.

Step Three

Select “Erase iPhone/iPad”

Then tap Erase iPhone/iPad again. enter your Apple ID password and tap Erase.

Step Four

Select Remove to remove the device from your iCloud account once the device has been erased.

That’s it! A confirmation will be sent to the email address associated with your iCloud account once you have successfully removed your device from iCloud.

Steps for accessing from your desktop or non-Apple device

Accessing from an Apple mobile device?

Step One

Go to Find My iPhone

on https://www.icloud.com/#find

You may be prompted to log in with your Apple ID and password.

Step Two

Click All Devices

Then select the device that you traded in.

Step Three

Click “Erase iPhone/iPad”

Then click Erase again. Enter your Apple ID password and click Done. Because the device isn’t lost, don’t enter a phone number or message.

Step Four

Click Remove from Account once the device is erased.

That’s it! A confirmation will be sent to the email address associated with your iCloud account once you have successfully removed your device from iCloud.
